## COLD EMAIL BEST PRACTICES

### Subject Line Testing:
- Keep it under 50 characters
- Use their business name for personalization
- Focus on THEIR problem, not your solution
- Avoid spam triggers: "Free," "Limited time," "Act now"

### Body Copy Rules:
- Under 150 words
- One clear CTA
- Local angle (Santa Clarita, SCV, local business)
- Proof element (even if it's just "I'm local, not overseas")
- Make it about THEM, not you

### Timing:
- Send Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10am or 2-4pm local time
- Avoid Monday mornings (overwhelm) and Friday afternoons (checked out)
- Follow up 3 days later, then 7 days later, then stop

### Lists to Target:
1. **Auto repair shops** (high missed call rate)
2. **HVAC / Plumbing** (after-hours emergency calls)
3. **Salons / Spas** (appointment-heavy, always on phone)
4. **Gyms / Fitness studios** (membership inquiries)
5. **Dentists / Medical offices** (front desk overload)
6. **Restaurants** (reservations, catering inquiries)
7. **Home services** (landscaping, pool service, handyman)

### How to Scrape Emails:
1. Google Maps search: "[Business Type] Santa Clarita"
2. Visit website, find contact page
3. Use Hunter.io or Apollo.io for bulk email finding
4. Or manually: most SCV small business emails are: info@, contact@, or firstname@businessname.com

### Email Tools:
- **Lemlist** or **Instantly.ai** for cold email campaigns (avoid Gmail for bulk)
- **GMass** if you want to use Gmail (lower deliverability but easier)
- Warm up your domain first (send 10-20 personal emails before blasting)

## SUCCESS METRICS TO TRACK

Track these for EVERY outreach campaign:

1. **Emails sent**
2. **Open rate** (aim for 40%+)
3. **Reply rate** (aim for 10%+)
4. **Demo clicks** (should be 5-10% of opens)
5. **Calls booked** (should be 30%+ of demo clicks)
6. **Close rate** (target 30-50% of calls booked)

If open rate is low: fix subject line
If reply rate is low: fix body copy (make it shorter, more personal)
If demo clicks are low: CTA isn't clear enough
If calls aren't booking: demo bot isn't doing its job (fix the script)
